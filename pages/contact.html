Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries

Do you offer bulk orders for businesses?

Yes, we provide bulk ordering options for businesses such as restaurants, hotels, and retailers. Please contact our sales team at sales@harnammasale.com for more information on bulk pricing.

How can I check the status of my order?

Once your order is processed, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also contact our customer support team for order status updates.

What is your return policy?

We have a 30-day return policy for unopened products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service team to initiate the return process.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select international destinations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please contact us for specific information about shipping to your country.
