How It All Began

Founded in 2025 by Shri Harnam Singh, our journey began in a small spice shop in the bustling markets of Delhi. What started as a passion project quickly evolved into a beloved brand trusted by households across India.

  • Authentic family recipes
  • Carefully sourced ingredients
  • Traditional blending methods
  • Rigorous quality control

Shri Harnam Singh

Founder & Visionary

Our founder's vision was simple: to share the authentic flavors of India with the world through carefully crafted spice blends using traditional methods and the finest ingredients.

His legacy continues to inspire us as we maintain his commitment to quality, authenticity, and the rich culinary heritage of India.

"Quality is never an accident; it is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, intelligent direction and skillful execution."

Our Values

Principles that guide everything we do

Quality

We source only the finest quality spices from traditional spice-growing regions, ensuring each product delivers exceptional flavor and aroma. Our sourcing team travels across India to find the best spices:

  • Hand-selected ingredients
  • Rigorous quality testing
  • Premium grade spices only
  • Regular supplier audits

Authenticity

We remain true to traditional spice blending techniques, preserving the authentic flavors that have been cherished for generations. Our commitment to authenticity includes:

  • Traditional grinding methods
  • Family recipes preserved for generations
  • No artificial additives or preservatives
  • Small-batch production for quality control

Passion

Our passion for spices drives us to continuously explore and innovate, while maintaining the traditional essence that makes our products special. This passion is reflected in:

  • Ongoing research into flavor profiles
  • Culinary collaborations with chefs
  • Educational programs on spice use
  • Development of region-specific blends

Sustainability

We believe in sustainable practices that benefit our farmers, our customers, and our planet. Our sustainability initiatives include:

  • Fair trade partnerships with farmers
  • Eco-friendly packaging solutions
  • Reduced carbon footprint in production
  • Support for sustainable farming practices

Our Process

From farm to your kitchen - the journey of our spices

1. Sourcing

We carefully select the finest spices from traditional spice-growing regions, ensuring superior quality and authentic flavor.

2. Processing

Our spices undergo rigorous cleaning and sorting to ensure only the best quality makes it to the next stage.

3. Blending

We follow traditional recipes and techniques to create perfectly balanced blends that capture the essence of Indian cuisine.

4. Packaging

Our spices are carefully packaged to preserve their freshness, aroma, and flavor until they reach your kitchen.
