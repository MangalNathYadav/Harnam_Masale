Our Story

Bringing Traditional Flavors to Modern Kitchens

Founded in 1985 by Shri Harnam Singh, our journey began in a small spice shop in the bustling markets of Delhi. What started as a passion project quickly evolved into a beloved brand trusted by households across India and now exported to over 20 countries worldwide.
