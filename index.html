Our Story

The Journey of Harnam Masale

Celebrating authentic flavors since 1985

How It All Began

Founded in 1985 by Shri Harnam Singh, our journey began in a small spice shop in the bustling markets of Delhi. With a profound knowledge of traditional spice blending techniques passed down through generations, Harnam Singh set out to create authentic spice blends that would bring the true essence of Indian cuisine to every kitchen.

What started as a small family business with a handful of signature blends has now grown into a trusted name in the spice industry, offering an extensive range of premium quality spices and blends that are exported to various parts of the world.

Our Values

Principles that guide everything we do

Quality
